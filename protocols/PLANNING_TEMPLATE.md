## Loop Prevention

**Planning Limits:**
- Max 2 plan revisions before execution
- Max 3 review iterations
- If planning exceeds 10% of total time, STOP and execute

**Progress Indicators:**
Every planning session must produce:
- [ ] Clearer problem statement
- [ ] Better success criteria
- [ ] Improved approach
- [ ] Actionable phases

**If no progress indicators checked, it's a loop. STOP and execute with current plan.**
